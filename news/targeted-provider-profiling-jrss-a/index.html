New Publication: Targeted Provider Profiling in JRSS-A

October 9, 2025

Our paper Doubly robust nonparametric efficient estimation for healthcare provider evaluation is now published in JRSS-A. We propose doubly robust estimators based on Targeted Minimum Loss-Based Estimation (TMLE) for indirect standardization parameters relevant to healthcare provider profiling.

This was joint work with Yiting Li, Mara A. McAdams-DeMarco, Iván Díaz, and Wenbo Wu.
