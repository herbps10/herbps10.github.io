Presentation: Migration Projections at the Wittgenstein Center Conference, Vienna

November 20, 2025

I presented joint work with Adrian Raftery on “Projections of Refugee and Asylum Seeker Populations by Country of Origin and Destination” at the Wittgenstein Centre Conference 2025 on “Demographic Perspectives on Migration in the 21st Century”. Part of the work we presented has now been published in Demography. The event took place at the Austrian Academy of Sciences in Vienna.
